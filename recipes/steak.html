Steak Recipe

Juicy Steaks on the Grill

This is a recipe for steaks on the grill. Perfect for a summer bbq or even a winter respite

Ingredients

  • 2 Steaks
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat
  2. Drizzle olive oil all over the steaks. Then sprinkle salt on both sides of the steaks and rub in
  3. Once the grill is preheated, throw the steaks on the grill. Flip once after 4 minutes.
  4. Cook steak until reaches desired temperature then take off the grill. Let it rest for 5 minutes then enjoy!
