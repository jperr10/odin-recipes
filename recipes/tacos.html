Juicy Beef Tacos

These tacos are perfect for a Tuesday, or any day of the week. This is a simple beef taco recipe that can easily be adjusted to your liking.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound ground beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Chopped onions
  • Chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Brown the meat
  2. Add in other ingredients and stir
  3. Assemble the the tacos
